Events at Bonterra
A Venue as Distinctive as You Are

With a rich tradition of hosting exceptional events, Bonterra Trattoria provides a stunning setting for both intimate gatherings and grand celebrations. The interior, exuding timeless elegance, is highlighted by intricate chandeliers and a charming glass-enclosed wine cellar, complemented by a cozy rustic stone fireplace. Our Tuscan-inspired private terrace offers the perfect escape, allowing guests to step outside, unwind, and experience our hidden oasis.

Booking Information

For more details or to discuss your upcoming event, contact our Event Manager at 403.262.8480 or via email at events@creativeri.com.

During peak seasons, please allow up to 72 hours for us to confirm availability.
