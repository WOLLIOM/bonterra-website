Patio reservations

Patio tables can be reserved through our OpenTable booking system. There's a firm two-hour dining limit on outdoor tables, and the patio is weather-dependent. Parties over 12 for lunch, or over 6 for dinner, will be expected to commit to a minimum spend or be seated inside.

Large parties & events

For group patio dining or private events, contact our events planner directly at events@creativeri.com, or see our Events page.
