Since the room was built

Chandeliers, a glass-enclosed cellar, a stone fireplace that's warmed a thousand winter dinners — the room does a lot of the talking before the food even arrives.

The kitchen keeps to a Mediterranean line: bold, unfussy flavours, local ingredients, nothing over-worked. In warmer months the Tuscan-style terrace opens up like a second dining room.
