Our Story

Bonterra Trattoria was born from a dream to bring the authentic taste of Tuscany to Calgary. Our founders, inspired by the rolling hills of Chianti and the warmth of Italian family kitchens, created a space where every meal feels like a celebration.

We source the finest local ingredients, respect traditional techniques, and infuse each dish with a Mediterranean twist. From our handmade pastas to our wood‑fired specialties, every plate tells a story of craftsmanship and passion.

“La cucina è amore” – Cooking is love.

We believe that great food brings people together. Whether you're joining us for a romantic dinner, a family celebration, or a casual meal with friends, we promise an experience that feels like home — the Italian way.
